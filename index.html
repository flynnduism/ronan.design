I was born in Ireland in 1983, and via a dial-up modem and a lot of photoshopped band posters, I found my way to being a designer of screen-based things. I graduated from DCU with a BSc in Multimedia in 2006 and spent my twenties working in digital design agencies and startups in Dublin, before moving to California in 2013.

Two cats and two kids later, I live in Point Richmond. I've spent about sixteen years working across interaction, UX, branding, marketing, and product design roles, and more recently as an engineer working in open source tech.

Now I'm part of Fermyon Technologies — an exciting new entity.
