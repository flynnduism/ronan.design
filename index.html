I was born in Ireland in 1983, and via a dial-up modem and a lot of CD burning, I found my way to being a designer of screen-based things. I graduated from DCU with a BSc in Multimedia in 2006, and spent my 20s working in digital design agencies and startups in Dublin, before moving to California in 2013 and getting married.

Two cats and two kids later, I live in Point Richmond, CA. I've spent the last nine years specializing in front-end, branding and product design work for Engine Yard, Barricade, Deis and most recently, Microsoft Azure.

Now I am a part of Fermyon Technologies — watch this space!
